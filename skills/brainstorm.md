# Brainstorming Skill

When asked to brainstorm ideas, follow the "AI Amplified Ideation" process:

## Step 1: Seed (You)
- Write 3 rough ideas manually (analog or simple text)
- No filtering — raw, unpolished thoughts

## Step 2: Expand (AI)
- For each seed idea, generate 10 variations
- Explore adjacent possibilities
- Include wild/unconventional angles

## Step 3: Evaluate (AI + You)
- For each variation, analyze:
  - MVP complexity (1-10)
  - Market size (TAM estimate)
  - Your unfair advantage (1-10)
  - Time to first revenue
  - Competitive landscape

## Step 4: Combine (AI)
- What happens if we combine idea A with idea B?
- Cross-pollinate across domains
- Look for novel intersections

## Step 5: Validate (AI)
- Build a simple landing page or prototype
- Create a pitch in 3 sentences
- Identify 5 people to validate with

## Step 6: Decide (You + AI)
- Score each idea on: Effort × Impact × Excitement
- Pick top 3 for further exploration
- Create 30-day experiment plan for #1

## Output Format
- Final shortlist with scores
- Recommended first experiment
- Risk mitigation for top pick
- 30-day action checklist
